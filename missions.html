Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun in our solar system and the only one known to support life. Often called the "Blue Planet" due to its vast oceans of liquid water, Earth has an atmosphere primarily composed of nitrogen and oxygen, essential for sustaining life as we know it. Its surface is diverse, featuring continents, oceans, and a variety of ecosystems, making it a unique world within our solar system.
